## Version Policy

### Semantic Versioning
- **MAJOR**: Breaking changes that require migration
- **MINOR**: New features, backward compatible
- **PATCH**: Bug fixes, backward compatible

### Release Schedule
- **Patch releases**: As needed for critical fixes
- **Minor releases**: Monthly or when feature sets are complete
- **Major releases**: Quarterly or when breaking changes are required

### Support Policy
- **Current major version**: Full support and updates
- **Previous major version**: Security patches only
- **Older versions**: No support (upgrade required)
